Reading This week we discussed "Traveling While Trans" from Costanza-Chock, S. (2020) Design Justice: Community-Led Practices to Build the Worlds We Need. What I took away from this is that although it may often come up that airport travel is a nightmare in itself, it is an entirely different ballgame for people who are Transgender. Not only do they face discrimination in a place that is deemed as public or of use to everyone, but the subtle feeling of not belonging is a prevalent thing that I have noticed. In the article, they talked about how the system operator was confused on whether to press the pink or blue button to signify gender. I feel like systems need to have bigger upgrades now that he world is waking up and becoming more inclusive. The system is well known for enforcing misogynistic ideals of what society should look like but those ideals are of the past. Costanza-Chock, S., 2020.
